About Speaker

Christopher Russo is the superintendent of Riverdale School District in Portland, Oregon. He is an experienced International School Director and Public School Assistant Superintendent with a demonstrated history of growing enrollment, stabilizing finances, and streamlining curriculum and instruction. Proficient in K-12 Education, Current Staff Development/Educational Practices, Fundraising, Board Relations, Community Outreach all with the purpose of equitable outcomes. Currently hold a US Superintendent certification and in process to completing an EdD in curriculum and instruction.
